COMMUNITY

This is the place where a life-chat system is available to ask questions and to share experience during the pilot-test classes on a daily basis for any doubt which may arise on its usage.

Moderated by the trainers in each partner country the community is specially designed space for the participants in the courses.

The “new-born old” community will therefore have a place to actively learn by doing, self-educate and share opinions about actual problems. Elders participating to the community have a go on overcoming their digital resistance and fears, increasing their awareness about active ageing and actively get accustomed to “think and act digitally”, reading e-newspapers, practicing the newly acquired e-skills
